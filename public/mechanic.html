How the mechanic thinks

He’s not here to be impressed. He’s here to find the bill that’s hiding behind the photos.

He distrusts perfect listings.
Overly polished photos + vague description often means “don’t look too closely”.
He cares about history.
No records = assume the worst until proven otherwise.
He watches for mismatch.
Trim badges, odometer vs wear, mods vs claim, seller language vs reality.
Non-negotiables
  • ✓You stay anonymous. Screenshots aren’t stored after the report is generated (design goal).
  • ✓No scraping. We only analyse what you provide.
  • ✓No fence-sitting. The mechanic picks a side and tells you why.
Best practice for best results

Use **3 screenshots** minimum: (1) title + price + km, (2) description text, (3) photo grid / key photos. More context = sharper call.
