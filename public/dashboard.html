Verdict: Proceed with Caution

High-value vehicle with exceptional resale potential, but heavily modified. Immediate verification of engineering certificates (VASS/Mod Plate) is required for road legality in Australia. Listing accuracy is poor regarding drivetrain specification.

Regulatory (AU)

Mechanical Risks
