How It Works

From screenshot to blunt report in under a minute.

Listing Lens reads your screenshots like a fussy buyer’s advocate. It pulls out what matters, ignores the fluff, and flags the stuff that should make you walk.

Step 1

Grab 3–4 screenshots of the listing

Full page, price & kms, spec table, seller blurb. Think like you’re sending proofs to a mate you trust.

Step 2

Run it through Listing Lens

We parse the ad, cross-check it against model-specific pain points, and look for gaps between the story and the facts.

Step 3

Get a mechanic-grade verdict

You get a score, a risk rating, red flags, questions to throw at the seller, and a plain-English “should I even bother?” summary.

Questions

What Listing Lens is—and isn’t.

Is this a replacement for a pre-purchase inspection? ›
No. Listing Lens is a pre-filter. It helps you decide what’s worth booking an inspection for. If the report says “walk away”, you save the inspection fee for a better candidate.
What kind of listings can it handle? ›
Anything with wheels or an engine on a mainstream classifieds platform: cars, utes, 4x4s, bikes, caravans, boats, trucks and machinery. If a human can read the ad, Listing Lens can too.
Does it search the web or pull in live recalls? ›
Not in the MVP. Reports are based on the screenshots you upload plus known model patterns. In later versions we’ll add optional recall and pricing feeds, clearly labelled and sourced.
Will this slow my search down? ›
No. Most reports complete in under a minute. The idea is to kill obvious time-wasters early, so you spend more time driving good candidates and less time messaging about junk.
Can I save reports and come back later? ›
Yes. The dashboard keeps an archive of your scans. You’ll be able to compare vehicles side-by-side, revisit old listings, and export reports when you need to justify a decision.
