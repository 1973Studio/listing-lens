Common questions

Does it work with screenshots?

Yes. Upload a clear full-page screenshot. We read the visible details and compare against common risk patterns.

Is the price estimate accurate?

It’s a range based on similar km, trim, and year. It’s designed for negotiation leverage, not a formal valuation.

What markets are supported?

Australia first (Carsales, FB Marketplace, Gumtree). Other regions later.

Will it do houses and tech later?

Yes. Cars now; more categories once car reports hit quality and retention targets.
